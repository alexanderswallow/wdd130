About Me

Hi, I'm Alex Swallow

I'm a student at BYU–Idaho pursuing a degree in Cybersecurity. I have a strong interest in how things work, whether that's software, circuits, or mechanical systems. I enjoy building things from the ground up.

Outside of school I spend a lot of time in the garage working on classic trucks. I'm currently in the middle of a full build on a 1973 Ford F250 Highboy, swapping in a 460 big block engine and rebuilding the drivetrain from scratch. It has taught me a lot about patience, problem solving, and reading technical documentation. These skills translate surprisingly well to coding.

I'm taking WDD 130 because I want a solid foundation in web fundamentals before moving into more advanced courses. Understanding how HTML and CSS actually work not just copying templates, is something I care about. I hope to use these skills both professionally and for personal projects down the road.
