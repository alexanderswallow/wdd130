Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Dry Oar Boating website is to build awareness and revenue for our guided white water rafting vacation business. The site will showcase our river trips, camping packages, and rapids experiences while giving visitors an easy way to book an adventure. Our goal is to inspire outdoor enthusiasts to choose Dry Oar for an unforgettable experience on the water, and to convert site visitors into paying customers through compelling visuals, clear trip information, and a straightforward booking process.

Audience

Our target audience is adventure-seeking adults between the ages of 25 and 55 who have the income and schedule flexibility for leisure travel. They are outdoor enthusiasts who enjoy hiking, camping, and active vacations. Many are families planning a group experience, or couples and friend groups looking for an adrenaline rush away from daily life. They typically access the web on mobile devices while researching trips, and use desktop computers when making final booking decisions. They want clear trip details, transparent pricing, and confidence that the experience will be safe and exciting.
